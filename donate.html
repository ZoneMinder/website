Donate to ZoneMinder

Thank you for considering a donation.

Your donations go a long way to help us keep ZoneMinder free and awesome. You can contribute funds to the project, or directly fund specific features and bugs. We accept donations via BountySource, Paypal, Patreon, Github Sponsors, crypto currencies or just about any other way you can think of.

Please note that small donations are almost pointless. The processing fees take a significant chunk. If you can't make a donation of $5 or more, please save your money until you can.
